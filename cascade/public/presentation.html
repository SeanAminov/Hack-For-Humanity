The Solution

Real-time AI emergency
analysis & guidance

  • Describe any emergency — fire, earthquake, flood, chemical spill, and more
  • Get instant, actionable guidance: what to do NOW, safety warnings, evacuation routes
  • See nearby resources on a live map: hospitals, fire stations, shelters, police
  • Update the situation as it evolves and get refreshed analysis in real time
  • All powered by publicly available data — real protocols, real resources, real locations
